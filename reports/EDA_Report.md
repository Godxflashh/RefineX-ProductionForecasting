# Exploratory Data Analysis

Dataset:
- U.S. Energy Information Administration (EIA)

Observations:
- 399 monthly records
- 57 refinery production variables
- Time span: 1993–2026

Target Variable:
- U.S. Refinery Net Production of Crude Oil and Petroleum Products (Thousand Barrels)

Reason:
This represents the overall refinery production and is the most suitable KPI for forecasting overall production trends using Prophet.
